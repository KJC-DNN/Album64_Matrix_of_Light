# Album 64 - Matrix of Light

Album 64 is a high-energy, prophetic worship record that confronts false religion, cultic violence, cultural deception, and spiritual matrices with uncompromising biblical language and triumphant Christ-centered proclamation. Blending hard rock, orchestral textures, global instrumentation, and choral declarations, the album moves listeners from lament and confrontation to worship, healing, and hope in Jesus Christ.

### Prayer

Gracious God, our hearts ache with the world’s pain: cultic cruelty, terror, sickness, and fear. By Your mercy, break every false altar and every matrix that enslaves human hearts. Heal the wounded, protect the vulnerable, and grant us wisdom to pursue truth with humility and compassion. May this music point people to Jesus—the only true Savior—and may our lives reflect His justice and love. Amen.
